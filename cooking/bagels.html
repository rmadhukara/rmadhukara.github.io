bagels.

ingredients.

  • 2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 1/2 cup warm water
  • 3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour, plus more for kneading
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon salt

Top with (optional)

  • 1/4 cup almond milk
  • Everything bagel seasoning

procedure.

  • Mix the flour, salt, sugar, and yeast in a large bowl. When adding the yeast, try to avoid contact with the salt since salt kills yeast.
  • Make a well in the middle of the mixture, and start adding the water in 1/2 cup increments. Mix well until a smooth dough forms. If it is too dry, add 1 tablespoon of warm water at a time as needed. If it is too wet, add 1 tablespoon of flour at a time as needed.
  • Flour a countertop or large cutting board, and knead the dough for 10 minutes, or until it is smooth and elastic.
  • Wet the inside of a clean large non-metal bowl with water, place the dough inside, and turn to coat until the outside is moist. Cover with a damp dish towel and set it to rise in a warm place for 1 hour, or until it has doubled in size. Once risen, punch down the dough and set it aside for 10 minutes.
  • Cover a baking sheet with parchment paper and set aside. Remove the dough from the bowl, and divide the dough into 8 pieces. Roll each dough piece into a ball.
  • Coat your finger in flour, then press into the center of ball to form a ring. Stretch the dough balls into a rings about â…“ the diameter of the bagel, then place onto the baking sheet. Cover with a damp towel and let it sit for 10 minutes.
  • Preheat your oven to 425ËšF, and bring a large pot of water to a boil. Get your seasonings and almond milk ready as you will need that next.
  • Once the water has boiled, reduce the heat to medium, and use a slotted spoon to lower as many bagels that fit comfortably into the water. Once they to float to the top (a couple seconds), boil for 2 minutes, flip, then boil for another 2 minutes.
  • Once all the bagels have boiled, transfer them to your lined baking sheet. Brush the dough with almond milk, and sprinkle with your seasoning.
  • Bake the bagels 20 minutes, or until golden brown. Remove from oven and cool on a wire rack for at least 10 minutes. Store in an air tight container for 4-5 days.

notes.

  • Makes eight bagels. Feel free to cut the recipe in half.
  • I like eating with either vegan or non-vegan store bought cream cheese.
  • My favorite bagel sandwich is some arugula (or lettuce), avocado, green onions, pepper, and cream cheese.
